Who We Are

The year is 2026. We are a company of human beings who make technology, examples and templates to follow, technical help and hackathon API leading at gaming and social media conventions, provide helpful solutions for creator events and help creators/creatives build their communities. Some of our products are the Parleystar browser extension and Archyved.

Indies and Creatives

We are pro-indies and pro-creatives and pro-artists. We attend many events every year and shake your hands and smile and tell you "Good job" at personal cost and discomfort to us.

Privacy/Security Lead

It seems like everyone wants you to sign up and you give all of your data and biometrics and upload a selfie and consent to all the cookies. This is not intrinsically bad -- we use many websites and apps that do so and business is business these days. We are an experimental example of what would happen if someone tried to do the opposite: make websites, browser extensions, and technology that have little to no Javascript, no tracking, no telemetry, no A.I., work in Ladybird, Ultimatum, Cromite, Hu, TOR, and newer browsers in different types of mobile devices and in VPNs. This experiment could fail, but at least you know that someone tried it.
